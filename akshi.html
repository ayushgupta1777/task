    Current Responsibilities

    1) Working as a Senior Lecturer & Consultant in the department of Oral Medicine, Diagnosis & Radiology since October 2022, which involves catering to patients with various oro-facial diseases and oral mucosal lesions including TMD's and Oral Pre-Cancer and Oral Cancer.


    2) Additionally, working as a Maxillofacial radiologist specifically on CBCT modality at the Centre for Advanced Imaging at ITS CDSR with reporting of multiple cases involving various aspects of Oro-facial pathologies, Endodontics, Periodontics, Oro- facial Trauma, Oral Implantology and Orthodontics


    3) Both Undergraduate and Postgraduate Teaching schedule which includes lectures, discussions and research work.


    4) Comprehensive research work in the field of Oral and Maxillofacial Radiology with specific interest in Cone beam CT and Orofacial Pain.


    5) Other specific work avenues include Research in the fields of Oral Medicine and radiology.


    6) Administrative workload as part of the departmental workflow.





    Professional Skills

    1) Attended a Comprehensive Workshop On Basics Of TMJ/TMD, Orofacial Pain, Sleep Medicine, Snoring & Obstructive Sleep Apnea For Dentists conducted by Dr. Davis Thomas as part of the Smile USA training program under the purview of American Academy of Orofacial Pain; further upgradation of skills in management


    2) Reporting and Interpretation of CBCT scans encompassing Endodontics. Periodontics, Trauma, Implantology and Implant Simulation, Salivary gland imaging. Airway Assessment. TMJ imaging and various bony and soft tissue lesions involving the maxillofacial region


    3) Forensic Odontology - Specific work on Age assessment using Condylar Cortication With Cone Beam Computed Tomography.


    4) Comprehensive diagnosis and management of diseases of oral and maxillo-facial region.


    5) Acquired knowledge and training in various Intraoral and Extra-oral Radiographic Procedures and Interpretation in the Maxillo-facial region including Salivary glands via conventional and digital imaging systems


    6) Interpretation of advanced imaging modalities such as CT, CBCT in the diagnosis of various Oral and Maxillofacial disorders


    7) Diagnosis and non-surgical management of Temporo-Mandibular Joint Disorders.


    8) Acquired skills in performing Incisional/Excisional/Brush biopsy procedure for the screening and diagnosis of lesions of the oral cavity.


    9) Recognizing Oral Manifestations of Systemic Diseases, their diagnosis & management.
